# Skip Slop Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 12, 2026**

## Data We Collect

Skip Slop collects minimal data to provide its service:

- **Location Data**: Used only to show nearby restaurants on the map. Your location is processed on-device by Apple MapKit and is never sent to our servers.
- **Community Notes**: When you submit a community note, the note text, note type, and associated restaurant are stored in Apple's CloudKit public database. No personal identifiers are attached to notes.

## Data We Do Not Collect

- We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal information.
- We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs.
- We do not sell or share any data with third parties.
- We do not use advertising frameworks.

## Third-Party Services

- **Apple MapKit**: Used for map display and restaurant search. Subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/).
- **Apple CloudKit**: Used to store and sync community notes. Data is stored in Apple's infrastructure. Subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/).

## User-Generated Content

Community notes are moderated for inappropriate content before becoming publicly visible. Notes require community votes to reach public visibility. You may report inappropriate content through the app.

## Data Retention

Community notes are stored indefinitely in CloudKit. Local data (saved restaurants) is stored on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

## Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue at [github.com/ukaia/Skip-Slop](https://github.com/ukaia/Skip-Slop).
